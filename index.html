Who are we?

Founded in 2019 by group of friends, Onest has come a long way from its beginnings in Los Angeles. When our team first started out, their passion for making Internet better place drove them to quit day job so that WebSec can offer you the world's best cyber security solutions. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own agency.
